About Us

Our Story

HA Import and Export PLC is a premier coffee export facilitation company based in the heart of Ethiopia's coffee region. With decades of combined experience in the coffee industry, we specialize in sourcing the finest Ethiopian green coffee beans and facilitating their export to global markets.

Our company was founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and sustainability. We work directly with local farmers and cooperatives to ensure fair trade practices and the highest quality products.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To connect the world with Ethiopia's finest coffee while supporting sustainable farming practices and empowering local communities.

Vision: To be the leading facilitator of premium Ethiopian coffee exports, recognized for quality, reliability, and ethical business practices.

Our Services

Coffee Export Facilitation

We facilitate coffee exports under licensed exporters, handling all documentation, compliance, and logistics to ensure smooth transactions from Ethiopia to your destination.

Supplier Relationships & Contracts

We maintain strong relationships with farmers and cooperatives, negotiating fair contracts that benefit both producers and international buyers.

Quality Control & Certification

Our rigorous quality control processes ensure that only the finest beans meet our standards. We facilitate all necessary certifications for international trade.

Logistics & Packaging Solutions

We provide comprehensive logistics support and premium packaging options to preserve coffee quality during transit and storage.

Our Products

Premium Green Coffee Beans

We offer a variety of premium Ethiopian green coffee beans including Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Harar, and Limu, known for their distinct flavors and aromas.

Jute Bags Packaging

Traditional jute bags for coffee packaging, offering breathability and protection while maintaining environmental sustainability.

Polypropylene Bags

High-quality polypropylene bags for enhanced moisture protection and longer shelf life during transportation and storage.

Market & Impact

Global Demand for Ethiopian Coffee

Ethiopian coffee is renowned worldwide for its unique flavor profiles and high quality. The global demand continues to grow as consumers become more discerning about coffee origins and quality.

We facilitate exports to key markets including Europe, North America, and Asia, connecting Ethiopian farmers with international buyers.

Sustainability Practices

We are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and support the livelihoods of coffee-growing communities.

Community Impact

Through fair trade practices and direct relationships with farmers, we ensure that communities benefit from the global coffee trade, supporting education, healthcare, and infrastructure development.
